Past Projects

CareerSoul (2022)

This project aimed to design the UI for a mobile application that eases the job-hunting process for University students. Extensive user and market research was conducted to frame the problem succintly. Lo-fi and Hi-fi prototyping were done in Excalidraw and Figma respectively. Qualitative Think Aloud Interviews and System Usability Scale (SUS) were used in evaluating the end prototype.

Kueh Quest! (2022)

Working together with the Central Provident Fund Board for my final-year project in SUTD, my group created a web-based game aiming to educate elderly CPF members in navigating CPF's e-services. I contributed in user research, conducting interviews and focus group discussions with our target users as well as UI design and front-end development of the game, using Figma and ReactJS.

SingHealth Retail Audit App (2021)

This web application, developed in collaboration with SingHealth, aimed to provide a platform for SingHealth staff to conduct regular audit checks on the various tenants within SingHealth institutions. I contributed to the front-end of the web app using ReactJS, Tailwind CSS and AntDesign. To enhance the robustness of the web app, I contributed to the implementation of automated unit-testing with Selenium.

s(NO)oze (2020)

This Android alarm app aimed to gamify the experience of waking up in the morning - users have to complete a simple game to turn off the alarm. I contributed to the front-end development of the app as well as alarm functionality in Java, allowing me to deepen my knowledge of OOP principles and design patterns.
